# 2Q NHẪN THUẬT — Brand Information

## Brand Overview

**2Q Nhẫn Thuật** is a handcrafted jewelry and accessories brand based in Da Nang, Vietnam, specializing in transforming vintage spoons into unique wearable art.

Every piece is individually handcrafted, making each creation truly one-of-one. No two products are exactly alike.

The brand combines craftsmanship, sustainability, storytelling, and contemporary street culture, creating jewelry that carries both character and history.

---

# Brand Name

**2Q Nhẫn Thuật**

### Meaning

"Nhẫn Thuật" can be understood as "the art of ring making" or "the craft of transforming metal into meaningful wearable objects."

The name reflects the philosophy of turning ordinary objects into unique pieces of personal expression.

# What We Create

### Handmade Spoon Rings

Signature rings crafted from vintage spoons.

Each spoon carries its own shape, pattern, and history, making every ring unique.

### Spoon Bracelets

Hand-shaped bracelets created from stainless steel spoons.

Comfortable, durable, and suitable for daily wear.

### Custom Jewelry

Personalized rings and bracelets made according to customer preferences.

Possible customizations include:

* Ring sizing
* Bracelet sizing
* Shape adjustments
* Pattern selection
* Personal requests
* Custom engraving (if available)

### One-of-One Pieces

Every product is handcrafted individually.

No mass production.

No identical copies.

Each piece exists as a single unique item.

# Craftsmanship

All products are:

* Handmade
* Individually shaped
* Hand polished
* Carefully finished
* Made in small quantities

Each piece goes through a manual crafting process that preserves the original character and details of the spoon while transforming it into wearable jewelry.

Because every spoon is different, slight variations are considered part of the product's uniqueness.

---

# Design Philosophy

### Wearable History

Every spoon begins with a story.

Rather than melting materials into new forms, 2Q Nhẫn Thuật preserves existing patterns and transforms them into jewelry.

### Sustainability

Repurposing vintage spoons reduces waste and gives forgotten objects a new life.

### Individuality

No two people are the same.

No two rings should be either.

### Handmade Over Mass Production

The imperfections of handmade craftsmanship are embraced as part of the product's identity.

# Short Brand Description
